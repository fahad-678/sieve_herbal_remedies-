# Privacy Policy - Sieve Herbal Remedies

**Last Updated:** May 3, 2026

## Data Collection
Sieve Herbal Remedies does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties. All data is stored locally on your device only.

## Data Stored Locally
The app stores the following information on your device using SharedPreferences:
- **Favorites**: A list of herb IDs that you mark as favorites
- **Tracker Logs**: Timestamped entries you create in the wellness tracker

This data is stored only on your device and is not accessible to anyone else unless you manually share your device or extract the app data.

## Permissions
The app requires no special permissions beyond standard app storage.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use:
- Analytics or crash reporting
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage or sync services
- User accounts or login systems
- Location services
- Camera, microphone, or contacts access

## Data Security
Your data is protected by your device's built-in security. We recommend using device protection (PIN, fingerprint, or face unlock).

## Future Changes
If we add network features, analytics, cloud sync, or other data collection in future versions, this privacy policy will be updated before deployment.
